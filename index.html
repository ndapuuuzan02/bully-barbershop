Meet The Professional: Eugene Reyes

With over a decade of experience behind the chair, Eugene founded The Bully Barber to create a unique space in Moose Jaw where traditional barbering techniques meet contemporary style. His passion for the craft is matched only by his dedication to his clients, ensuring every person who walks through the door leaves looking and feeling their absolute best.

Eugene specializes in everything from classic hot towel shaves to the latest trends in perms and textured cuts, all within a studio environment that values quality, culture, and community.

Common Questions About Barbers in Moose Jaw

To ensure the highest quality of service and no wait times, we operate by appointment only. Please use our online booking system to secure your spot.

We kindly request at least 24 hours notice for any cancellations or rescheduling. This allows us to offer the appointment time to another client.
